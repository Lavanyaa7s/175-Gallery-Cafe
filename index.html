OUR STORY & HERITAGE

Where Heritage Shophouse Meets Artisanal Craft.

Nestled along the storied Jalan Tun Tan Cheng Lock in Melaka, 175 Gallery transforms a centuries-old Peranakan heritage shophouse into a living exhibit of gastronomy and coffee artistry.

"We believe that dining is an intimate dialogue between space, tradition, and culinary innovation."

Every beam of weathered wood and every sun-drenched courtyard stone tells the story of Melaka's vibrant trading past, while our baristas and chefs craft contemporary flavors that awaken the senses.
